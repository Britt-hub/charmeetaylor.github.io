About Me

Charmeé Taylor (she/her) is a bisexual actor, writer, producer, and content creator. Her work centers bisexual visibility, storytelling, and authentic queer experiences — especially for people discovering their identity later in life.

She is the creator of Confessions of a Bisexual, an interactive memoir for baby gays, and produces content exploring relationships, identity, and LGBTQ+ culture.

Originally from State College, Pennsylvania and now based in Los Angeles, Charmeé is passionate about creating stories that celebrate queer joy, curiosity, and community.

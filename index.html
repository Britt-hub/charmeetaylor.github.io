Charmeé Taylor

Actor • Writer • Producer • Content Creator

Charmeé Taylor (she/her) is a proud bisexual actor, the author of Confessions of a Bisexual: An Interactive Memoir for Baby Gays and content creator behind bi_astrology and The Good Bi Podcast.

Originally from State College, Pennsylvania, she studied Drama at Pennsylvania State University and is now based in Los Angeles.

Her work focuses on relationships, coming out later in life, and bisexual representation in TV and film.
